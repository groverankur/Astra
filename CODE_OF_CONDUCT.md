# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Astra to be a collaborative, respectful, and technically rigorous project.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent while still being honest about problems
- welcome questions from contributors at different experience levels
- focus feedback on behavior, code, design, and decisions rather than people

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- doxxing or unwanted disclosure of private information
- repeated disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions
that do not align with this standard.
